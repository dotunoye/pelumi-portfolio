The Visionary Behind Carnelian Realty

Oyedotun Pelumi is the CEO of Carnelian Realty, a luxury real estate and property development firm with deep expertise in premium asset acquisition and portfolio management. With a keen eye for emerging markets and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Oyedotun has built an investment portfolio spanning Lagos, Abuja, and beyond.

His thought leadership in luxury real estate development, sustainable construction, and high-net-worth client relations has established Carnelian Realty as a premier developer in West Africa's luxury market.
